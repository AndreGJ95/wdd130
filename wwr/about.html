About Us - White Water Rafting Adventures

Founded in 1995, White Water Rafting Adventures started with just two rafts and a dream. Over the years, we have guided thousands of guests down our beautiful rivers.

Today, Exciting Expeditions offers a variety of trips ranging from gentle scenic floats for families to adrenaline-pumping class V rapids for seasoned adventurers.

We are proud of our reputation for excellent customer service, environmental stewardship and for creating experiences that last a lifetime.

Our History

Since our beginnings in 1995, we have grown from two rafts to a fleet serving thousands of adventurers.

From family floats to extreme class V rapids, our history is built on passion, safety, and unforgettable experiences.
